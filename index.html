Samosas

Description of Samosas. Samosas are a staple appetizer, snack, and street food in Indian cuisine. Indian samosas can be filled with various ingredients, from spiced peas and potatoes to chicken to chickpeas. No matter what's inside this crispy and deep-fried dough, no samosa is complete without its complementary sauce.

Biryani

Description of Biryani. Biryani is a mixed rice dish, mainly popular in South Asia. It is made with rice, some type of meat and spices. To cater to vegetarians, in some cases, it is prepared by substituting vegetables or paneer for the meat. Sometimes eggs and/or potatoes are also added.
